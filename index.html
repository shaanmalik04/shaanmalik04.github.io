Biography

Dr. Benyamin Ahmadnia is an Assistant Professor of Computer Science. His main research interests are Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing, and Machine Translation. Dr. Ahmadnia received his Ph.D. from the Autonomous University of Barcelona (Spain) in 2017. He is the Associate Editor of Open Computer Science (De Gruyter), Editorial Board member, and PC/Technical member of several scientific journals and international conferences and workshops worldwide.
